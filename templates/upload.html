How to import trades

  • 1. Open Tradovate (web) and go to "Reports" on the right hand side Menu.
  • 2. Select the account you want to export from in the drop list on the left.
  • 3. From the [Performance] tab, select the dates and Download CSV.
  • 4. Drop your CSV file here.
  • 5. Before you import, make sure you have the right account selected on the top left.
  • 6. Click "Preview" to preview you trades to import.
  • 7. Import your trade with the button at the bottom of the table.


The fees configured in you settings will be applied.
If no fees are found, $0 will be applied and you can edit them later.
